How long before your SSD dies

Current year:
Purchase year:

Drive Health: (TBW)
Output: NaN years

Steps explained

1. Getting years of usage.

Formula: Current year - Purchase year
Example: 2023-2018 = 5 years

2. Getting how many times the endurance will last.

Formula: TBW (Endurance) / Drive Health 
Example: 600/17 = 35 times.

3. Use years of usage and endurance times.

In the final step, we produce the years needed to kill your SSD by usage. 
Formula: endurance times * years of usage
Example: 35 * 5 = 176 years
